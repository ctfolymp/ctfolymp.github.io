Independent CTF 2019

Last year winners: Lincoln Hypes 97, Marilynn Diem 94, Joeann Saechao 93

Independent CTF 2019 - open CTF competition for people of all ages from all countries of the world.

For the third year now, we have been organizing an independent CTF, there is no prize pool in this competition, it was created to give an opportunity for a hacker from all over the world to measure his capabilities.

This year we will organize the 3rd Independent CTF 2019. This will take place in 3 stages, in the first stage we choose the 100 best participants who will be admitted to the second stage. In the second stage, we select 10 people who will be admitted to the finals. In the final, you can get for one task not only 0 or maximum score, each solution will be individually evaluated from 0 to 10 points, after which we will choose the winners.
Each stage takes place in 4 rounds and each participant is judged by his best result in these 4 rounds.

Competition consists of three stages
  • 1st stage (4 rounds)
  • 2nd stage (4 rounds)
  • The final (4 rounds)
